FEATURES

Grobage Spacer

Grobage spacer is used to allocate the spcae for NGO’s or for a common man who wants to plant tress.With this incredible tool, we will be able to connect the person who desperately wants to fill up his space with green but has no time to do so or maintain it and a volunteer who wants some space to grow trees for the welfare of humanity. We provide them a platform with which they can contact each other and share their thoughts.when the land is far away from the house and they still want to grow some trees. In this case, the local volunteers will help them to fill up the land with green. Through this platform, they can just connect and share more and more regarding the plantation.Planting trees not only increases the oxygen level or environment stability but also makes the soil fertile and increases the ground water level.

Grobage Forum

This features helps the common man, botanist, NGOs or the volunteer to connect and ask their doubts or raise a query. This is more like a traditional forum where people used to ask their doubts or raise a query, but the activists will be there to help you 24x7 and this dedicated forum will have some FAQs, so you can go there and find your related queries or else you can just search using keywords and sort out the related queries or doubts, if you didn’t find any, you can raise your own and people will be able to answer the queries. You can go through the queries raised by your neighbourhood and you can go and help them in personal also or you can just find out common disease spreading in your locale and make measures accordingly.

Track

Tracking stuff ain’t unusual, we also have our dedicated tracking feature where one will be able to track their tress growth, either the person who has volunteered to take care your trees will post the images of your trees or you can request a video calling session or photos of the trees. After sending the request, the volunteer will send the photos or will arrange for a video call. After this you can ping the request as satisfied and you can rate your plantation(scale 1-5). Through this platform, every year we will be able conduct some competition and reward prices for the best plantation, it doesn’t matter how big the plantation is, its all about the involvement of the volunteer and work done in that particular plantation.
